About Us

Who We Are

Kerry Painters consists of Garry Eager and John Breen, who together have a total of 35 years painting experience in Ireland, England and Europe. We are local, based in Tralee and our family roots are based in Tralee and Blennerville.

What we do

We are trained in a wide range of painting techniques and skills from varnishing old oak bar furniture to painting modern interiors t patching up old exteriors and applying a beautiful finishing coat to really improve it visually. We specialize in Interiors, Exteriors and Commercial work. We are fully tax compliant and fully insured. We also have licenses and insurance to operate plant hire machinery for bigger jobs or hard to access areas .

What to expect

  • Being punctual. We will be on site at 8, or earlier if required and will stay until the end of the day.
  • A professional job. Before painting we will treat the walls with fillers, touch up any loose or broken plaster, have the surface perfect to take the paint and apply the pefect finish for the colours that you chose.
  • A very competitive price, which we will have agreed upon beforehand, no hidden extras, everything is transparent.
  • As a professional outfit we always leave our worksace pristine and always carry our left over products and packaging away.
  • Fully insured and tax compliant, we are professionals, so we guarantee a quality product with no risk

What not to expect

  • Tardiness, turning up late and leaving early.
  • Rushed jobs, painting over cracks and dirt.
  • Being overcharged, hidden costs or unagreed extras.
  • A mess, paint on the floor and decoror rubbish left behind
  • Cowboys
